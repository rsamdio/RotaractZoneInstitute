About RZI 2026

Empowering the next generation of Rotaract leaders

The Rotaract Zone Institute 2026 Chennai is a comprehensive leadership development program that brings together Rotaract leaders from across the zone for an intensive four-day experience.

Through interactive workshops, keynote presentations, networking opportunities, and hands-on projects, participants will develop essential leadership skills, expand their professional network, and gain insights into effective community service.
